Delicious Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Serving Size: 8-10 people

Ingredients

  • 2 cups All-purpose flour
  • 2 cups Granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp Baking soda
  • 1 tsp Baking powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup Vegetable oil
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 cup Hot water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add buttermilk, oil, eggs, and vanilla to the dry ingredients; beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in hot water until combined (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a wooden skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes before inverting onto a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Frost as desired (e.g., with chocolate buttercream).
